Between PR2 & the RTM of 4.5 the functionality of auto-matic spell on send
changed. In PR2 if you hit stop the mail was sent, in RTM if you hit stop the
mail is unsent. This is extreamly painful since you now have to spell the
complete document before you can send it. At a minimum you should have added a
"Done" button so you can abandon the spell check and still send.....

I'm cc'ing you to make sure you're aware of this mail/news feature requirement
on the Ender spell checker:

It used to be that clicking the Stop button stopped spell checking but sent the
mail. That seemed bad, so we changed it so that the Stop button also stops
sending the message. It's now apparent that we need two buttons: one to stop
spell checking and send anyway, and another to cancel the whole thing.
