    No, I'm not going to do that.

Bugzilla has too many fields and parameters as it is.  I'm trying to keep it as
simple as possible.

You can do things you need to with the existing target milestone field, even if
it isn't ideal for you.  Too many projects don't know what their future version
numbers will be, or change them at the last minute; that's why Bugzilla doesn't
do the "target version" thing.  But you can just assign an equivilance between
milestones and planned future versions, if you like.
