    There are already three options for sorting - importance, bug num, assignee.
"Last changed" would be a useful addition.  This would allow me to monitor
changes when I have web but not email access, since the top bug in query
results would be the one most recently changed, and hence the first one to look
at.

There is already the ability to restrict to bugs changed within the last x days,
but this is not coarser than a day (I'm asking for sorting based on date &
time), and does not put the most recently changed at the top.

    I'm not disagreeing, and I'm not closing the bug, but you should realize that
there is a way to get the functionality you asked for.

Bring up a list of bugs.  Click on "change columns".  Make sure that you have
checked "changeddate".  Click on Submit.

Now, in your new list of bugs, click on the "Changed" column header.  The list
of bugs will be sorted in order of change.

And the most recent one is on the bottom, not the top, but that's pretty easy to
live with...
