    You may not want this "enhancement", but it seemed to
me that you might want to whine about ASSIGNED bugs which
have not changed.  And also about REMIND and LATER bugs.
So I expanded the concept of whining to include these.

Note: The modifications were designed to minimize the
differences between the official Bugzilla and my version.
Thus the 'whinedays' parameter was not changed to
'whinedays_new'.  And the whining was all left in 'whineatnews.pl'.
You may wish to change this if you accept the enhancement.

I have some questions about the design and operations philosophy
behind the RESOLVED REMIND and RESOLVED LATER bug states,
but will address those on the discussion group.
