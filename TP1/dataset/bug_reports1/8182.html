    I don't know if the Verdana font has been updated to support the Euro.
According to Apple's Technote 1140 - The Euro Currency Symbol
    http://developer.apple.com/technotes/tn/tn1140.html#OS Support

Mac OS Fonts
 The standard European glyph, as designed and specified by the European Union in
 Brussels, has been added to all of Apple's Mac OS fonts to be distributed with
 Mac OS 8.5, due out later in 1998. Under Mac OS 8.5, you will find the Euro
 glyph in the following fonts: Apple Chancery, Capitals, Charcoal, Chicago,
 Courier, Gadget, Geneva, Helvetica, Hoefler Text, Monaco, New York, Palatino,
 Sand, Skia, Symbol, Techno, Textile, and Times.
