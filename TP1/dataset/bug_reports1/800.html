    This bug actually came from the rdf backend.  When you clicked on "add
bookmark", the rdf backend reloaded the page through libnet in order to get the
meta tags.  The reload caused libnet to try to display the page with a bogus
context.  I see that HT_LayoutComplete now gets a list of metatags and doesn't
have to do the page reload hack anymore.
Marking fixed.
