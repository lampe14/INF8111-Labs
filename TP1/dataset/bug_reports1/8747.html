    Crashes at startup are an occasional fact of life under NT, and (for us, at
least) have historically been easy to fix. Search for all files that match
"moz*.dat" and delete them. Delete the x86rel folder. Re-unzip the files and
launch apprunner.exe. Create a profile (if you don't it will crash). You may
crash at this point; if you do, relaunch and you should be good to go.

In short, after basic troubleshooting, I have no crashes at launch under NT 4 SP
5 using the 1999062808 M8 build, and I have had no startup crashes under NT for
some time.
