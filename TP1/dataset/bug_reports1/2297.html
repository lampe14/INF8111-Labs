Unless Bijal objects, you can use this short description of CCK:


CCK

    Client Customization Kit 5.0 is a collection of tools that enable
distributors to customize
    Communicator 5.0 and create installers for download or for CD distribution.
Included in
    the kit are:

        CCK Wizard - make the most popular customizations and create your
installer from
        this single wizard.
        CD Shell - present your branding and catch your customer's attention
when they first
        put your CD in the drive.
        Account Setup Wizard - automatically configure Communicator for dialup
access to
        an ISP account.

    CCK 5.0 will be developed as free source.  Projects are already underway.
See
    http://www.mozilla.org/projects/cck for more information.
